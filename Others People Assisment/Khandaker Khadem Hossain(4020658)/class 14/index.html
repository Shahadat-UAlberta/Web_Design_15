About Us!

Bangladesh is situated in south-east Asia.She borders on India and Myanmar in the east,on India in the north and west,to the south,lies the Bay of Bengal.Dhaka is our capital city.The total land area of the country including rivers is 1,47,579 square kilometres.

Bangladesh is called a land of rivers as she is criss-crossed by hundreds of big and small rivers.Of them the Padma,the Jamuna, the Meghna,the Brahmaputra and the Karnaphuli are our main rivers.We have many good poets in our country. Kazi Nazrul Islam,Michael Modhusudan Dutta,Jibananda Dash,Begum Sufia Kamal and Shamsur Rahman are our famouspoets.Kazi Nazrul Islam is our national poet.
